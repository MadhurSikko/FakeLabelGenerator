Baked Goods & Biscuits (Tests leavening agents, emulsifiers, and added sugars)
Britannia Good Day Cashew Cookies (Britannia) - Tests how the AI parses nut allergens and butter/oil blends.
Britannia NutriChoice Digestive Biscuits (Britannia) - Good for testing dietary fiber claims and complex carbohydrate breakdowns.
Ready-to-Cook & Instant Foods (Tests heavy use of functional classes)
Maggi 2-Minute Noodles (Masala) (Nestle) - The holy grail of ingredient lists. It tests the AI on flavor enhancers like INS 627 and INS 631, thickeners, and palm oil.
MTR Ready-To-Eat Palak Paneer (MTR Foods/Orkla) - Tests the AI on retort-pouch packaging labels and sterilized food regulations without heavy preservatives.
Condiments, Sauces & Spreads (Tests pure preservatives and stabilizers)
Knorr Classic Thick Tomato Soup (HUL) - Packed with thickeners, anti-caking agents, and dehydrated vegetables.
Amul Pasteurized Butter (GCMMF) - A very short, straightforward dairy label (just butter, salt, and annatto color) to see if the AI overcomplicates simple items.
Beverages (Tests artificial sweeteners, synthetic colors, and caffeine warnings)

Sunfeast Yippee! Magic Masala Noodles (ITC) - A strong comparison point to Maggi with slightly different vegetable and spice powder compositions.
Sunfeast Dark Fantasy Choco Fills (ITC) - Contains complex emulsifiers (like INS 322) and artificial flavoring substances.
Bingo! Mad Angles Tomato Mischief (ITC) - Excellent for testing how the AI handles "permitted natural colors."
Haldiram's Aloo Bhujia (Haldiram's) - A baseline for traditional Indian snacks with standardized regional ingredients.
Kurkure Masala Munch (PepsiCo) - Contains specific acidity regulators and flavor enhancers.
Lay's India's Magic Masala (PepsiCo) - Great for testing spice extracts and complex flavoring agents.
Parle-G Original Glucose Biscuits (Parle) - A perfect, simple baseline label with minimal complex additives.
Kissan Fresh Tomato Ketchup (HUL) - The standard for testing Class II preservatives like Sodium Benzoate (INS 211).
Thums Up (Coca-Cola India) - Tests the AI on mandatory caffeine warnings and caramel color (INS 150d).
Slice Mango Drink (PepsiCo) - Great for testing the distinction between actual fruit pulp percentage and added synthetic flavors.
Real Fruit Power Mixed Fruit Juice (Dabur) - Tests the AI's ability to read complex, multi-fruit reconstitution ratios and acidity regulators
Paper Boat Aamras (Hector Beverages) - A good test for "clean label" claims that still use specific FSSAI-approved stabilizers.
Cadbury Dairy Milk Chocolate (Mondelez India) - The benchmark for testing cocoa butter equivalents, milk solid percentages, and PGPR (INS 476) emulsifiers.
Nestle KitKat (Nestle) - Tests the AI on separating the wafer ingredients from the chocolate coating ingredients within the same label.
